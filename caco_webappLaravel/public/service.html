Our Services

Explore Our CACO Services

1. Nutrition Counseling

CACO offers personalized nutritional counceling services to individuals,providing tailored guidance on dietary choices and healthy living practices. Trained professionals work closely with clients to empower them with knowledge and strategies for improving their overall health through natural means.
2. Community Workshops on Healthy Living

CACO organizes informatives workshops within the community,focusing on varous aspects of healthy living. These workshops cover topics such as nutrition,physical activity, and mental well-being,fostering a collective understanding of holistic health practices among community members.
3. Nutrition Counseling(Reiterated)

Through repeated emphasis on nutritional counseling,CACO ensures ongoing support for individuals seeking guidance on their dietary habits. This sustained effort aims to reinforce postive lifestyle changes and promote long-term health bebefits for participants.
Distribution of fruits and natural foods

CACO actively engages in distributing locally sourced fruits and natural foods to the community. This initiative not only enhances access to nutritious options but also encourages the consumption of locally available,sustainable resources,contributing to the overall well-being of individuals in the region.
